Re:Incarnation (2024)
Silicone, electronics, found materials (obsolete devices) - 3 x 1.5m

Experimental installation combining sculpture and new media art to investigate the intersection of bodily presence and technological interface. Through silicone lifecasting, obsolete devices and embedded electronics, the project centers on creating striking, sensorial and haptic pieces, fusing cast body parts with technological objects to generate hybridized forms that question the thresholds of embodiment in a post-digital world.
